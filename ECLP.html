Plants:

The Peace Lily Plant

scientifically known as Spathiphyllum, is a popular indoor plant celebrated for its elegant, white, sail-like flowers and glossy, deep green leaves. Peace Lilies are known for their air-purifying properties, helping to improve indoor air quality by removing pollutants. They thrive in low to moderate light conditions and are a a cherished choice for homes and offices.

Price: $228

The Sago Palm Plant

The Sago Palm (Cycas revoluta) is great for anyone who's looking for a low-maintenance but interesting houseplant. This charming, ancient-looking plant can bring a touch of the tropics to your indoor or outdoor spaces. So long as it's not over-watered, it should thrive in most conditions.

Price: $182

The Variegated Croton Plant

This variegated croton gets its name from its thin, twisty leaves, which are dappled in shades of red, yellow, green, and pink. The more light it gets, the brighter and more varied the colors become. To that point, this tropical plant requires bright direct sun. South- and west-facing windows are best. Sized to ship best, our large Corkscrew Croton arrives with room to grow as it adapts to your home’s conditions.

Price: $210
